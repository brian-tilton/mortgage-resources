Review Factors We Consider

Every lender is scored using the same transparent framework. Here are the key factors:

  • Rates & Pricing: Competitive interest rates, APRs, and overall loan costs compared using current market data, HMDA disclosures, and live quoting tools.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Aggregated verified Google reviews, Trustpilot, BBB, J.D. Power studies, and recent borrower testimonials. Emphasis on communication, responsiveness, and closing experience.
  • Transparency with Costs & Fees: How clearly and completely lenders disclose all fees, points, and closing costs upfront on their websites and in Loan Estimates. Hidden fees or last-minute surprises lower scores significantly.
  • Willingness to Educate Borrowers: Quality of educational resources, rate explanations, first-time homebuyer guidance, and how well borrowers report feeling informed throughout the process (no high-pressure tactics).
  • Industry Knowledge & Expertise: Years in business, licensing, regulatory compliance, range of products (conventional, FHA, VA, jumbo, etc.), and ability to handle complex scenarios. We also review expert third-party analyses.
  • Additional Important Factors: Speed of pre-approval and closing, quality of online tools and mobile experience, customer service availability (phone/chat/email), flexibility with credit profiles and special programs, and overall reputation/complaint history.
