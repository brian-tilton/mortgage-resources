Rocket Mortgage Review 2026

Overall Rating: 4.5 / 5

Overview

Rocket Mortgage (formerly Quicken Loans) is a major player known for its technology-driven process and wide availability. It scores well on rates and digital tools but receives mixed feedback on transparency and personalized service compared to our top pick.

Key Ratings

  • Rates & Pricing: Very Good — Competitive rates, especially for FHA loans.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Very Good (4.5/5 on Trustpilot with 40k+ reviews; lower on some platforms like Zillow ~3.3/5).
  • Transparency: Good — Clear online tools but some complaints about unexpected fees or changes.
  • Education: Good — Extensive resources and app, but can feel less personal.
  • Industry Knowledge: Very Good — Strong FHA and conventional options; fast pre-approvals.

Google & Customer Reviews Summary

Trustpilot: 4.5/5 (40,891 reviews). Many praise the app and speed. Some note communication issues or rate changes at closing.

ConsumerAffairs: 4.0/5. J.D. Power has recognized them highly in past servicer studies.

Sources: Trustpilot, Zillow, ConsumerAffairs, J.D. Power (2025-2026 data).
